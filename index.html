I am a Colombian psychologist, certified music therapist, and Biodanza facilitator based in Denmark. What makes my work different: I don't just teach — I embody what I offer. I sing, play guitar, dance, and bring the resilience and joy of my Latin American roots into every room I enter. Biodanza — the methodBiodanza — metoden

More than dance —
a system for human integration Mere end dans —
et system for menneskelig integration

Biodanza was developed in the 1960s by Chilean anthropologist Rolando Toro. It combines music, movement, and group encounter to activate "vivencias" — moments of heightened presence and aliveness. No performance. No steps to learn. Biodanza blev udviklet i 1960'erne af den chilenske antropolog Rolando Toro. Det kombinerer musik, bevægelse og gruppesamvær for at aktivere "vivencias" — øjeblikke af skærpet nærvær og livfuldhed. Ingen præstation. Ingen trin at lære.

The method works through the nervous system — using rhythm and shared movement to reduce isolation, build resilience, and restore a felt sense of joy. As one participant put it: "fællesskab på individets præmisser" — community on each person's own terms. Metoden virker gennem nervesystemet — rytme og fælles bevægelse reducerer isolation, opbygger robusthed og genopretter en oplevelse af glæde. Som en deltager satte ord på det: "fællesskab på individets præmisser."
